About ylearner

ylearner is a free coding education platform by Algorid Limited, built with a single mission: make professional programming education accessible to everyone, regardless of background or budget.

Our Mission

Programming is one of the most valuable and in-demand skills in the modern world. Yet high-quality education is often locked behind expensive courses, boot camps, or university tuition. ylearner changes that.

We believe every aspiring developer — a student in a small town, a career-changer looking for a better path, a professional expanding their skill set, or a self-taught coder anywhere in the world — deserves access to the same quality education.

Our Content Standards

Every lesson on ylearner is written following these principles:

  • Accuracy — All code is tested and verified with Python 3.10+
  • Clarity — Explanations use plain English, not unnecessary jargon
  • Completeness — Each lesson covers the topic in full, not just the easy parts
  • Practicality — Real-world examples and applications throughout
  • Currency — Content is updated to reflect modern Python best practices
